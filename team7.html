Internet Usage Habits Survey

Understanding Student Internet Usage Patterns

Survey Overview

The Internet Usage Habits Survey aims to gather data on how students use the internet and its impact on their daily lives. This survey is designed to provide insights for educators and policymakers to develop strategies for promoting healthy internet use among students.

Survey Objectives:

  • To determine the frequency and duration of internet use among students.
  • To identify the most common online activities and platforms used by students.
  • To assess the impact of internet use on students' academic performance and social relationships.
  • To provide insights for educators and policymakers to develop strategies for promoting healthy internet use among students.
