ICT OR STEM CAREER INTEREST

TEAM CHAOS: CAREER INTEREST

Survey Overview

Greetings! This survey is designed to collect valuable insights regarding students' interests in pursuing careers in Information and Communications Technology (ICT) and Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) fields. It aims to understand the extent of awareness, motivation, and inclination among students toward these fast-evolving and in-demand areas of study and profession.

Survey Objectives:

  • To promote self-awareness among students about their personal goals and interests.
  • To understand what careers students are aiming for and why.
