Social Media Usage and its Effect on Mental Health

Welcome to Our Survey!

Introduction: This survey explores the relationship between social media usage and students' mental health. With the rise of digital platforms, it's important to understand how online habits impact emotional well-being, focus, and daily life.

Objectives:

  • ✔️ To examine the frequency and purpose of social media use among students.
  • ✔️ To assess the emotional and mental effects of prolonged social media exposure.
  • ✔️ To identify signs of stress, anxiety, or isolation linked to online behavior.
  • ✔️ To raise awareness about healthy social media habits and mental well-being.
